## Bojan's Test Kitchen Uses:
- **Google Gemini 2.5 Pro**
  <ul className="no-bullet">
    <li>+ Great for most things, especially vision and reasoning.</li>
    <li>- Slow when reasoning, okay price.</li>
  </ul>
- **OpenAI GPT-5 Medium Reasoning**
  <ul className="no-bullet">
    <li>+ Amazing at web research, great all-arounder.</li>
    <li>- Not as clever as Gemini 2.5 Pro, slow when reasoning.</li>
  </ul>
- **Qwen3 235B A22B**
  <ul className="no-bullet">
    <li>+ *Really* cheap, very fast, pretty clever, available with reasoning.</li>
    <li>- Not as clever as Gemini 2.5 Pro, not multi-modal, small context window. </li>
  </ul>
- **OpenAI GPT-5 Low Reasoning**
  <ul className="no-bullet">
    <li>+ Coding: Free*, fast, good at using tools and coding</li>
    <li>- Not quite as smart of a coder as Sonnet 4</li>
  </ul>
